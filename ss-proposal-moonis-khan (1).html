ePB · Back Office Software
ePB Lite™ — Entry-level price book and store automation. Integrates with tobacco, candy, Coke, Pepsi, beer/wine vendors. Price changes that used to take days now take minutes.
ePB Premium™ — Full-functioned, enterprise-wide price book and store sales reporting for multi-site operators.
ePB Books™ — Robust store automation with price book, fuel, and lottery management. Wet stock management, computer-aided ordering (CAO), daily reconciliation, inventory control, store merchandising, and comprehensive analytics. Can improve store revenue by 3–12%.
ePB Accounting™ — All ePB features plus integration with third-party G/L systems. Most popular with QuickBooks and Sage users.
ItScans — Handheld Inventory Management App
  • Receive Vendor Merchandise (DSD) — transmitted directly to ePB back-office for review, modification, or acceptance.
  • Add new products — Identify shelf items and transmit to ePB.
  • Manage all items at the shelf — Add or edit items, descriptions, departments, and prices.
  • Store Inventory Auditing — Blind count management with shrink trend analysis over time.
  • Price Validation — Check master price book against shelf prices for consistency.
  • Quantity on Hand — Validate actual inventory and generate shrinkage reports.
  • Create Purchase Orders — Reorder any shelf item through the handheld app.
  • Lottery Scratch Ticket Inventory — Use phone camera to count lottery tickets; inventory updates immediately in ePB™.
